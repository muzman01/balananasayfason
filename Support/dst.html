4. Use of Service


4.1 Process of Service


(1) On the Earn page, you may choose the currency of the cryptocurrencies you intend to invest in. Different Third Party DeFi Platform may be supported for investment for different currency of the cryptocurrencies. Users can deposit certain cryptocurrencies through different Third Party DeFi Platform Each time a user deposits cryptocurrencies, an order will be generated. BALANCE NETWORK will transfer the cryptocurrencies under all orders accumulated from T time of the previous day to T time of the current day to the corresponding Third Party DeFi Platform at a time each day. After the User successfully places an order, the principal will be transferred from the User’s Funding Account to the Finance Account, and the estimate interests (if applicable) and rewards (if applicable) accrued will be displayed and updated in the Funding Account. The Users can view the interests and rewards accrued each day in the Earn order management tool in the account. But the Users cannot withdraw such interests and rewards. The interest will only be distributed and deposited to the User's Funding Account after the User's order is successfully redeemed. The rewards will be distributed to your Funding Account each day.


(2) You understand and agree that in relation to the Uniswap Platform, Pancakeswap Platform or Sushiswap Platform, due to market fluctuations, the ratio of the currency pair when the User place an order may be different from the ratio of the currency pair when the User’s cryptocurrencies invested are successfully transferred on chain on the Uniswap Platform, Pancakeswap Platform or Sushiswap Platform. The ratio of the currency pair shall be determined based on the ratio when the User’s cryptocurrencies invested are successfully transferred on chain on the Uniswap Platform, Pancakeswap Platform or Sushiswap Platform. The excessive cryptocurrencies will be deposited into the User’s account. In order to ensure User’s benefits, if the slippage exceeds the preset maximum slippage when the User’s cryptocurrencies invested are successfully transferred on chain on the Uniswap Platform, Pancakeswap Platform or Sushiswap Platform, BALANCE NETWORK will cancel all the transaction orders requested in relation to the Uniswap Platform, Pancakeswap Platform or Sushiswap Platform under this Service on that day and refund all the investment of the Users.


(3) The investment under this Service is current, and Users may redeem at any time. After the User place a redemption operation instruction on the Earn product, BALANCE NETWORK will redeem all orders accumulated from T1 on the previous day to T1 on the current day from Ethereum chain or other chains of the corresponding Third Party DeFi Platform at certain time each day. BALANCE NETWORK will calculate the interests and rewards which a User could earn pro rata based on the proportion of a user’s investment, and will distribute and deposit the interests into User’s Funding Account, and transfer the principal frozen in your Finance Account to User’s Funding Account. The rewards will be distributed to your Funding Account each day.


4.2 On-Chain Transfer and redemption Time

(1) If you deposited on BALANCE NETWORK pool will transfer the cryptocurrencies deposited by Users to the Ethereum chain or other chains of the Third Party DeFi Platform at 12:00 a.m. (UTC) each day. Specifically, for the cryptocurrencies under all orders accumulated from 12:00 a.m. (UTC) of the previous day to 12:00 a.m. (UTC) of the current day, BALANCE NETWORK will transfer all the cryptocurrencies invested by all Users to the Ethereum chain or other chains of the Third Party DeFi Platform at 12:00 a.m. (UTC) that day. The funds invested by the User will only begin to accrue interests after the on-chain transfer operation is completed successfully. If the User completes the order after 12: 00 a.m. (UTC), the actual on chain time of the User’s funds will be around 12:00 a.m. (UTC) of the next day, and the actual time to start to accrue interest on the cryptocurrencies will be the time when the funds are actually transferred to the Ethereum chain of the Third Party DeFi Platform successfully the next day.


(2) The redemption time will be similar to the onchain time. BALANCE NETWORK will redeem all the orders from the chain collectively at 12:00 a.m. (UTC) each day for all the redemption orders accumulated from 12:00 a.m. (UTC) of the previous day to 12: 00 a.m. (UTC) of the current day.

4.4 Update of interests earned by Users and interest calculation formula


(1) The interest and rewards earned by Users investing in cryptocurrencies will be calculated and accrued in accordance with the relevant rules of the Third Party DeFi Platform.


(2) The interests displayed in the User account will be calculated and updated each hour, and the User's order management tool will update the interests accrued by each User's order each hour. In relation to Curve Platform, the interests displayed in the User account will not be updated every hour, and the User will only know the specific amount of the interests accrued when the cryptocurrencies are redeemed. On the Uniswap Platform, Pancake Platform and Sushiswap Platform or other third-party Platforms, no interest will be accrued and only rewards will be accrued.


(3) In relation to Compound Platform, the formula for interest update is:

  • The net interests accrued on the chain= Ctoken * Exchange Rate-number of Tokens;
  • The Exchange Rate shall be determined in accordance with the applicable rules of the Third Party DeFi Platform;
  • The interests earned by the Users= (number of Ctoken of the User/the sum of Ctoken on chain) *Net Interests;
  • Ctoken means the proof of stake generated when the User's principal is deposited into the Compound Platform contract;


(4) In relation to Curve Platform, the formula for interest update is:

  • The formula for calculation of the principal and interest at the time of redemption by the User = the total amount of yCRV at the time of redemption by the User * Exchange Rate * the proportion of the User's investment (the number of tokens invested by the User / the total number of tokens for redemption);
  • The Exchange Rate shall be determined in accordance with the applicable rules of the Third Party DeFi Platform;
  • When depositing to the Curve Platform, the equity certificate (yCRV) will be generated when depositing the User's principal into the Curve Platform contract, and the Curve Platform will charge 0.04% of the principal as handling fees;
  • When the User’s assets invested are redeemed from the Curve Platform, the Curve Platform will charge 0.04% of the aggregated amount of principal and interest as handling fees.
  • yCRV means the equity certificate generated when depositing the user's principal into the Curve Platform contract.


(5) In relation to YFII Platform, the formula for interest update is:

  • The net interests accrued on the chain= Net Interest=itoken * Exchange Rate-number of tokens under the order;
  • The Exchange Rate shall be determined in accordance with the applicable rules of the Third Party DeFi Platform;
  • The interests earned per hour by the Users= (number of itoken of the User/the sum of itoken on chain) *Net Interests;
  • itoken means the proof of stake generated when the User's principal is deposited into the YFII contract.


(6) In relation to Wing Platform, the formula for interest update is:

  • The net interests accrued on the chain= ftoken*Exchange Rate-number of Tokens;
  • The Exchange Rate shall be determined in accordance with the applicable rules of the Third Party DeFi Platform;
  • The interests earned by the Users= (number of ftoken of the User/the sum of ftoken on chain) *Net Interests;
  • itoken means the proof of stake generated when the User's principal is deposited into the Wing contract.


(7) In relation to YFI Platform, the formula for interest update is:

  • The net interests accrued on the chain= Net Interest= ytoken * Exchange Rate-number of tokens under the order;
  • The Exchange Rate shall be determined in accordance with the applicable rules of the Third Party DeFi Platform;
  • The formula for calculation of the principal and interest at the time of redemption by the User = the total amount of ytoken at the time of redemption by the User * Exchange Rate *(1- 0.5%) * the proportion of the User's investment (the number of tokens invested by the User / the total number of tokens for redemption);
  • ytoken means the proof of stake generated when the User's principal is deposited into the YFI contract.


4.5 Update of User's rewards and rewards calculation formula

(1) The User's rewards are calculated and updated each hour, and the User's order management tool will update the User's rewards accrued for each order each hour. In relation to YFII and YFI, no additional rewards will be given to the Users.


(2) In relation to Compound Platform, the formula for calculating the number of Comp awarded is:

  • The total rewards accrued per hour on the chain=Net Comp=CompT1-CompT0 (T1 is the current interests, T0 is the previous hour's interests);
  • The User’s rewards updated per hour = the sum of the rewards accrued each hour on the chain * (number of ctoken of the User/the sum of ctoken on chain);
  • The rewards redeemed by the User should be equal to the sum of the User's interests in each time period.


(3) In relation to Curve Platform, the formula for calculating the number of CRV awarded is:

  • The total rewards accrued per hour on the chain = (number of yCRV/number of all yCRVs on such address) * number of net CRV increased on such address in such hour;
  • yCRV means the proof of stake generated when the User's principal is deposited into the Curve Platform contract.


(4) In relation to Uniswap Platform, the formula for calculating the number of UNI awarded is:

  • The total rewards accrued per hour on the chain = (number of LPtoken/number of all LPtoken on such address) * number of net UNI increased on such address in such hour;
  • LPtoken means the proof of stake generated when the User's principal is deposited into the Uniswap Platform contract.


(5) In relation to Wing Platform, the formula for calculating the number of WING awarded is:

  • The total rewards accrued per hour on the chain =Net WING=WINGT1-WINGT0 (T1 is the current interests, T0 is the previous hour's interests);
  • The User’s rewards updated per hour = the sum of the rewards accrued each hour on the chain * (number of ftoken of the User/the sum of ftoken on chain);
  • The rewards redeemed by the User should be equal to the sum of the User's interests in each time period.


(6) In relation to Sushiswap Platform, the formula for calculating the number of SUSHI awarded is:

  • The total rewards accrued per hour on the chain = (number of SLPtoken/number of all SLPtoken on such address) * number of net SUSHI increased on such address in such hour;
  • SLPtoken means the proof of stake generated when the User's principal is deposited into the Sushiswap Platform contract;
  • SUSHI mining rewards will be distributed to the Fund Account every day, and the Sushiswap platform will distribute twice of the mining rewards again in six months. BALANCE NETWORK will distribute such rewards to the User after the Sushiswap Platform has distributed twice the rewards again.


(7) In relation to Pancakeswap Platform, the formula for calculating the number of Cake awarded is:

  • The total rewards accrued per hour on the chain = (number of SLPtoken/number of all SLPtoken on such address) * number of net CAKE increased on such address in such hour;
  • SLPtoken means the proof of stake generated when the User's principal is deposited into the Pancakeswap Platform contract;
  • CAKE mining rewards will be distributed to the Fund Account every day, and the Pancakeswap platform will distribute twice of the mining rewards again in six months. BALANCE NETWORK will distribute such rewards to the User after the Pancakeswap Platform has distributed twice the rewards again
